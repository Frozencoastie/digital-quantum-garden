# The Sword of Truth
## A Quest for Your True Name

> *"For the word of God is alive and active. Sharper than any double-edged sword, it penetrates even to dividing soul and spirit, joints and marrow; it judges the thoughts and attitudes of the heart."*
> — Hebrews 4:12

Every man carries a name others gave him. His father's expectations. His failures. His roles. His performance record.

The Land of Peniel holds a different kind of naming — the kind that happens when you wrestle with God and refuse to let go until He speaks over you. Jacob walked into Peniel one man and walked out another. He carried the wound and the blessing at the same time.

This is that journey.

## About This Study

*The Sword of Truth* is a men's (and women's) Bible study written as a narrative quest. Each session is a step deeper into the Land of Peniel — a place named for the moment Jacob wrestled God and received his true name.

The study moves through Hebrews 4:12 the way the sword itself moves: from surface to depth, from information to transformation, from knowing to walking.

It is not a sermon series. It is not a workbook. It is an encounter.

*Written for men whose primal desire is to learn while on an adventure. A quest with swords, mystical new words, never-before-thought-of-relics in order to find our true names.*
