# My Somatic Digital Garden

*Where embodied knowing takes root and ideas cross-pollinate across orchards of thought*

## What Grows Here

This garden contains multiple orchards — each cultivating a different aspect of integrated human knowing. Like the fruits of the spirit, these domains nourish different dimensions of wholeness.

I'm exploring what happens when we refuse the Western bifurcation of mind/body, soul/psyche, sacred/secular. When we return to **Nephesh** — the Hebrew understanding of the integrated human being.

## The Orchards

### 🌳 The Systems Orchard

*Bearing fruits of understanding and wisdom*

Completing Dana Meadows' work: What if **the Mind** is the missing 6th element of systems thinking? From quantum psychology to the evolution of consciousness and capitalism.

### 🕊️ The Nephesh Orchard

*Bearing fruits of peace and integration*

**The Sword of Truth** — An epic journey exploring how dividing soul from psyche fractures us. A biblical study on reclaiming wholeness. On finding what was never lost, only obscured.

### 🎸 The Creative Expression Orchard

*Bearing fruits of joy and beauty*

Heavy metal as sacred art. Ghost, Iron Maiden, and the symbolism embedded in music that speaks to truths words alone cannot carry.

### 🧠 The Neurodivergent Wisdom Orchard

*Bearing fruits of patience and kindness*

What neurodivergent minds are capable of when given space to think non-linearly. Gabor Maté's *The Myth of Normal* and the cost of forcing conformity.

### 🤖 The AI & Wholeness Orchard

*Bearing fruits of faithfulness*

AI as super-assistant for knowledge workers — not to replace our Nephesh but to protect it. How technology can reduce ungoverned cognitive load and return us to families, to ourselves, to life.

### 📚 The Scholarly Sources Orchard

*Bearing fruits of goodness*

The thinkers, researchers, and truth-seekers whose rigorous work illuminates the paths between orchards.

## How to Tend This Garden

- **Wander freely** — Cross-pollination happens at the edges
- **Follow the non-linear paths** — Connections emerge unexpectedly
- **Bring your whole self** — Mind and body, soul and psyche, sacred and secular
- **Return often** — Living systems grow
